About Me

Jev Suchoi has extensive experience as among others a Solution Architect, Cloud Consultant, Security Consultant, Tech Lead, Infrastructure Engineer, DevOps Engineer and Developer. In his work for different organizations he has accumulated extensive knowledge of Azure, Azure Active Directory and Azure DevOps. With a developer background Jev is able to fully incorporate development principles when designing and implementing cloud solutions. As an automation & security specialist he is a firm believer of the following concepts & principles;

  • Lifelong learning
  • Everything as Code
  • Automate the boring
  • Pets vs Cattle
  • Idempotency
  • Assume breach
  • Cloud-native security
  • Least Privilege

Currently Jev Suchoi is employed Rubicon: Cloud Advisor as a Principal Cloud Consultant. By performing this role Jev is responsible for the technology aspects of the Azure team of the Cloud Solutions cluster. The technology aspects include but not limited to;

  • Facilitating & organizing knowledge sharing in and outside of the team
  • Participating on the technology board
  • Participating in proposals for our customers with regards to Azure & Office 365
  • Translating Enterprise level designs into technical designs & implementation approaches
  • Conducting recruitment interviews with candidates

In additions to the official role Jev is considered by he’s colleagues as the go-to guy for Azure, scripting & Azure DevOps related questions who has a passion to share knowledge within the organization.
